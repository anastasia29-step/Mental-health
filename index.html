About Our
Therapists

Garcia & J. Smith Therapists offer specialized
therapy services focusing on grief, loss, and
acculturation issues. Our approach is rooted in
cultural sensitivity and community engagement,
providing a safe space for individuals and families
to heal and grow. We are dedicated to making a
positive impact on mental health and well-being.
